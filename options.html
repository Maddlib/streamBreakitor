Append or Replace:

Append adds the unstreamed String to the end of the page. This does not go away without loading a new page/refreshing the current page. Navigating to a new tab in SPOC, including by searching for a new result, WILL NOT CLEAR the string.

Replace breaks pages by removing what was there and replacing the entire page with a new string. This is great for monitor, because the pages are essentially stand alone, but very much breaks SPOC functionality.
